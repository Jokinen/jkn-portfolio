Wallpaper database

  • 6/2013 — 8/2013

The focus with this site was to introduce an alternative to the then popular blog solutions for finding wallpapers.

User experience was enchanted with a streamlined interface which focused on giving the user the relevant controls when they needed them. The site also tries to do some common tasks for the user, for instance guess the size of their monitor and suggest the right size of wallpapers based on that.

Technologies used

Django, Python, Pillow, SQLite, Haystack, Scrapy, JavaScript, jQuery, entypo.

Frontpage

The goal with this site was to create a simple yet sleek interface for finding good quality wallpapers. I attempted to keep all the relevant controls there, but also keep them out of sight until needed as to make the user experience simple to start off.

Search

I wanted search to be the most important navigation component on the site. After focusing the search input field and typing your first keystroke, the search option bar animates into view. You can always pop up the menu yourself from the hambuger menu on the left.

I wanted to offer a service for a user who knew what kind of wallpaper they were after. Because of this I took the liberty to not keep the search options contantly in view. They rather pop up after you've begun your search to only give one strating point for the user to start off from. The filters enable you to limit the results to the ones suiting your computer and needs. This approach to the menu also makes it easier to optimize the site for mobile.

Now, later on, I wouldn't have grouped the "arrange by" option with the rest of the filters. On a very basic level these options do something different from the rest - they rearrange content instead of limiting it.

Wallpaper view

With great wallpapers the site is easy to make look good. I wanted the wallpaper view to focus on the wallpaper itself. You can fullscreen the image to see it in all of its glory.

For me the msot bothersome part of finding a wallpaper was the amount of trouble you needed to go through in order to preview it properly. Here I wanted to make it possible without having to navigate to a new page. The image is also draggable for those situation when it overlaps your browser window.

By contiuing to browse these screenshots, you'll se the different states for the wallpaper view.
